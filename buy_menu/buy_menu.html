Book overview:

Monster and hero: The Monster made of Flesh and Magic has been causing havoc at Townsville, who is brave enough to stop such calamity? Fear not citizens of Townsville, for the Hero is present, his skin made of bare metal, his eyes gleamed a bluish light, the speed of a cheetah, the strength of the rhinoceros, the intellect of a philosophist. Written by the famous Franklin Graham, follow the battles and reasoning of the Hero against the Monster, ending in a dramatic conclusion.
